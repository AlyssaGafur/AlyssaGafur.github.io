In The Beginning

We love Squidward and music!

Squidward recieves the oppurtunity to prove his musical skills to Squlliam Fancyson at the Bubble Bowl in the Bikin Bottom. He attempts to acquire a band full of Bikini Bottom residents, however a lack of patience and unconsistent practice leads the band to fail miserably. Coming to Squidward's rescue, Spongebob gathers all members of the band and encourages them to work hard for the sake of Squidward who has helped everyone in their times of need. As Squidward was prepared for embarrassment at the Bubble Bowl, the band surprises him, leaving him shocked and amazed, and also once again, proving himself to Squlliam Fancyson.
